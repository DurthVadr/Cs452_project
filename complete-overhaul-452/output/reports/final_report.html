Understanding the Metrics

  • Accuracy: Overall percentage of correct predictions
  • Precision: When the model predicts a win, how often it's correct
  • Recall: What percentage of actual wins the model correctly predicts
  • F1 Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall
  • Upset Metrics: Same measures but specifically for games where the underdog wins
